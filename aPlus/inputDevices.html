Keyboard

A device used to input commands into the computer.
Basic Function: Word processing, write letters, emails, visit web pages, etc.
Equipped with 2 different connector types: 6 pin din or USB (Many keyboards work with both due to Din/USB adapter)

Mouse

A pointing devices that fits in the palm of your hannd that replicates the movement of the mouse on the computer screen.
Basic Function: Starting applications, file and folder management, and surfing the internet
Will typically have 1 or 2 buttons (Macintosh - 1, Windows - 2)

Digital Camera

A device that takes digital pictures and videos
Stores images digitally on an internal hard drive or SD (Secure Digital) card.
Images can then be downloaded to the computer using USB cable or SD card. Once on computer images can be viewed or printed

Barcode Reader

A device used for reading barcodes
uses a light to scan barcodes
Typically used to detect point of sales and maintain inventory
Connects to computer with either a serial port, USB port, or wireless
Trigger type barcode is the most popular

Scanner

A device that enables you to put a digital copy of a physical copy on your computer

Touch Screen

LCD monitor with an input dectection system.
1 Click on a mouse button = 1 tap by a finger. 2 clicks by a mouse button = 2 taps by a finger

Web Cam

A device used for capturing video and images
Mainly used for video conferencing and video calling over the internet
Most webcams have a built in microphone and connect to the computer through a USB cable
Logitech and Microsoft are the biggest manufacturers of webcams

Media Card Reader

Give the computer the ability to read memory cards from devices such as cameras
Has slots to accomodate various sizes of memory cards (Micro SD and Mini SD cards)
Conects to computer via USB

Docking Station

Used for laptop computers
Used for turning a laptop into virtuall a desktop computer
Allows you to connect to exterior monitor, keyboard, and mouse
Docking stations are specific to the manufacturer. There is no one size fits all docking station

KVM Switch

A KVM switch allows you to control multiple computers from a single monitor, keyboard, and mouse
Each computer has an individual connection to the KVM
Saves money and space on keyboards / mouses / etc.

Smart TV

A television that can connect to the internet (Television combined with computer)
Many have built in webcam and microphone

Smart Card

A great way to prove the identity of a person
A smart card is the same size as a credit card with an embedded microprocessor chip

Smart Card Reader

Devices used to accept and read smart cards

Biometric Device

Used to prove the identity of a person by recognizing unique physical characteristics
For example: A fingerprint scanner

Motion Sensor

Records hand movement in mid air without your hand making any contact with the device
Connects to computer via USB

Digitizer

A devices that is used to convert analog movement, such as a hand drawing, into a digital form on your computer
Flat surface with a stylus. As you draw on surface with a stylus, it will draw on the screen
Connects via USB

Game Pads and Joysticks

Controllers used for gaming systems, like consoles
Connect to computers via USB
