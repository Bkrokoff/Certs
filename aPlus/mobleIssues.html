Mobile Issues (Phones and Laptops)

Touchscreen non-responsive:
Try restarting the phone
Eject an reinsert the SIM card
May have to do a hard reset

Apps not loading, freezing, and slow performance:
Too many apps open eating up memory, so close them
Restart your device

GPS not working:
Ensure the GPS is turned on in settings
Interference will cause GPS issues

Laptop Issues

Blank Screen:
Try connecting an external monitor
If image shows up, LCD monitor on laptop is broken

Faint Image:
Most likely you will have ro replace the inverter
Inverter - small circuit board that supplies power to the LCD monitor (Converts DC current to AC current)

Battery not charging:
Could be a defective battery or defective motherboard

Battery only lasts a few minutes before discharging:
Batteries lose their capacity to hold a charge as they get older

Flickering Monitor:
Can be related to a bad LCD monitor or inverter
If flickering happens when moving laptop, related to bad LCD or inverter

Sticky Keys:
Laptop keyboards are not as durable as regular desktop keyboards
Be extra careful when replacing keps on laptop

Ghost Cursor:
Disable tap feature in touchpad settings
Improper touchpad drivers. Download correct drivers

Overheating:
Vents and fans get blocked with dusts
Blow compressed air into vents and dust
Need to be placed a hard, flat surface

Intermittent Wireless Issues:
Interference between laptop and router
Out of range from the router
Wireless network hard drivers need to update
