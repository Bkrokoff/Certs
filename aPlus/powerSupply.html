Power Supply

Supplies power to the computers
Converts AC current into the specific voltages that the computer needs
ATX is the most common form factor that a power supply comes in

Power Supply Connectors

P1: Main power connector - 20 or 24 pins
P4: Supplies power to the CPU - 4 pins
Molex: For connecting hard drives and optical drives - 4 pins
SATA: Used to connect SATA drives - 15 pins
4 Pin Berg: Floppy drive connector - 4 pins
6 Pin: Supplies power to certain PCI-E video cards
